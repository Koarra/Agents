CLIENT PROFILE - HERITAGE ARTS INTERNATIONAL LLC

Company Overview:
Heritage Arts International LLC is a professional art brokerage and auction house based in New York, specializing in rare antiquities, fine art, and cultural artifacts from various civilizations. The company was founded in 2018 by Marcus Chen and operates galleries in Manhattan and Geneva.

Business Activities:
Our primary business involves the professional sale, brokerage, and auction of high-value art pieces including:
- Ancient Roman and Greek antiquities
- Renaissance paintings and sculptures
- Rare Asian art and ceramics
- Pre-Columbian artifacts
- Contemporary fine art from established artists

The company maintains climate-controlled storage facilities in Manhattan and operates a professional authentication laboratory. We conduct quarterly auctions with items typically valued between $50,000 and $5 million. Our client base includes private collectors, museums, and institutional investors globally.

Financial Information:
Annual revenue: $18.5 million
Primary income sources:
- Auction commissions: 65% ($12 million)
- Direct art sales: 25% ($4.6 million)
- Storage and authentication services: 10% ($1.9 million)

Marcus Chen serves as CEO and holds executive management position overseeing all acquisition and authentication decisions. He has 20 years of experience in art dealing and holds a Master's degree in Art History from Columbia University.

Notable Transactions:
Recent significant sales include a Greco-Roman marble sculpture ($2.3M), a Ming Dynasty vase ($1.8M), and several Renaissance paintings. All items come with complete provenance documentation and export/import permits as required by international law.

The company maintains comprehensive insurance coverage and follows all regulations regarding cultural heritage protection. We work closely with Interpol's database of stolen art and conduct thorough due diligence on all acquisitions.

Compliance:
Heritage Arts International is registered with relevant authorities and maintains anti-money laundering procedures. We verify the source of funds for transactions exceeding $100,000 and maintain detailed records of all sales and acquisitions.
