CLIENT PROFILE - GREEN HORIZON ENTERPRISES

Company Overview:
Green Horizon Enterprises is a vertically integrated cannabis business operating in Colorado, USA. The company was established in 2019 following the legalization of recreational cannabis in the state. CEO and founder Sarah Mitchell holds an executive management position and owns 75% of the company.

Business Operations:
Green Horizon operates direct cannabis cultivation, processing, and retail distribution facilities. Our operations include:

Cultivation Facilities:
- 50,000 sq ft indoor growing facility in Denver
- State-of-the-art hydroponic systems
- Produces approximately 2,000 pounds of cannabis annually
- Licensed by Colorado Department of Revenue (License #403-2019-GH)

Processing Operations:
- Extraction and processing laboratory for cannabis concentrates
- Production of edibles, vapes, and topical products
- Quality testing and compliance laboratory

Retail Distribution:
- Three licensed dispensaries in Denver metropolitan area
- Direct-to-consumer sales through retail locations
- Medical and recreational cannabis product lines

Financial Information:
Annual Revenue: $8.5 million
Revenue Breakdown:
- Direct cannabis sales (retail): $6.2 million (73%)
- Wholesale to other licensed dispensaries: $2.3 million (27%)

The cannabis-related activity represents 100% of the company's total income as cannabis operations are our sole business focus.

Compliance and Licensing:
Green Horizon maintains full compliance with Colorado state regulations:
- Colorado Marijuana Enforcement Division licensed operator
- All facilities inspected and approved by state authorities
- Strict inventory tracking using state-mandated seed-to-sale system
- Regular testing for pesticides, potency, and contaminants
- Employee background checks and licensing

Sarah Mitchell, as CEO, is directly involved in all cultivation, distribution, and retail operations. She holds required state cannabis business licenses and has completed mandatory compliance training.

Banking Relationships:
Due to federal banking restrictions, the company maintains accounts with cannabis-friendly state-chartered credit unions and operates with enhanced due diligence measures. We maintain detailed financial records and file all required state tax documents.

The company seeks banking services for payroll processing, vendor payments, and treasury management to support continued growth in Colorado's legal cannabis market.
